Zaryab Muhammad Akram

I am currently a Machine Learning Engineer at Epic Games, where I'm part of a research team focusing on 3D generation and reconstruction. Previously, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), graduating summa cum laude and receiving the President's Gold Medal. During my undergraduate studies, I worked as a research assistant at TUKL-SEECS under the guidance of Prof. Faisal Shafait and Prof. Muhammad Shehzad, contributing to projects at the intersection of computer vision and machine learning.
